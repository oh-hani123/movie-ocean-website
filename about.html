About Movie Ocean

Our Mission

To provide honest, detailed reviews that help movie lovers make informed decisions about what to watch next.

Our Review Process

  1. Watch the movie/TV series multiple times
  2. Take detailed notes on plot, acting, direction
  3. Discuss with team members
  4. Write honest review (no spoilers!)
  5. Publish with rating system
