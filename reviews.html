Latest Reviews

All the Bright Places

Type: Movie

Genre: Drama, Romance

Review: A emotional story about two teenagers dealing with mental health issues who form a deep connection. The film beautifully explores themes of love, loss, and healing while addressing depression and grief.

Why I recommend it: I think everyone should watch this to understand mental health better. It breaks down stereotypes about depression and shows how kindness and understanding can literally save lives.

Soulmate

Type: Movie

Genre: Drama, Fantasy

Review: A beautiful fantasy drama about connection and destiny. The movie explores deep relationships and the idea of soulmates in a touching and thought-provoking way.

My Neighbor Totoro

Type: Movie

Genre: Animation, Family

Review: A Studio Ghibli masterpiece that captures childhood wonder and imagination. This heartwarming story about two sisters and their magical forest friends is perfect for all ages.

Daily Dose of Sunshine

Type: TV Series

Genre: Drama, Medical

Review: An excellent Korean drama that follows nurses and doctors in a psychiatric ward. The series handles mental health topics with sensitivity and shows the importance of compassion in healthcare.

Why I recommend it: This series shows a powerful truth - depression can happen to anyone, even the people who are supposed to help us. But more importantly, it reminds us that no matter how dark things get, it will get better. The pain will pass, and there is always hope for healing.
